# Security Principles
## CIA Triad
Confidentiality-Integrity-Availability
- C: Access management (sensitivity)
- I: Accurate, consistent data (baseline and state)
- A: Timely + Reliable accessible (criticality)

Authentication
- Knowledge, Token, or Characteristic
